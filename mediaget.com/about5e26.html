Mengenai Syarikat
The Mediaget Company firstly appered in 2009 as a result of endeavors of a small developer group wishing to create an instrument for easy access to media content for everyone.
The Mediaget download manager appeared to be so easy to use that almost every person even those without deep IT knowledge were able to find music, movies or games very quickly.
During the following years were created powerful search algorythms, up-to-date catalogue with media content and easy-to-understand interface.  
Mediaget has become not just a torrent download manager, but also a mediaplayer which allowed users to watch videos and listen music just after download begins.
Mobile version of Mediaget allowed users to use it anywhere with their Android devices.
When developing Mediaget we take into account interests and demands of our users so you can enjoy user-friendly features and great potential of our software.
